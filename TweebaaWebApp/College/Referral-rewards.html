  • Referral rewards
  • Can’t wait to tell your friends about Tweebaa?
  • Refer your friends, family, neighbors, co-workers, and social networks to Tweebaa and you can earn special Referral Rewards!
    Each time you refer a new Tweebaa member who registers, you will receive 30 points in all three Zones! (See Zone reward points)
    · + 30 Points – Submit Zone for EACH referred member
    · + 30 Points – Evaluate Zone for EACH referred member
    · + 30 Points – Share Zone for EACH referred member

    Additionally, for each 10 new Tweebaa members you bring aboard at Tweebaa, we’ll give you 400 Shopping Reward Points! (See Shopping reward points)
    · + 400 Shopping Reward Points – for each TEN referred members

    You can receive Referral Rewards in two ways:
    1 – Tell your friends to register at www.tweebaa.com and make sure they list your email address (the one associated with your Tweebaa account) in the registration form. They’ll have to select that they were referred by “Friend” and put your email in the available box.

    2 – When you share Tweebaa products with your friends (via “Share & Earn” link), and your friend follows your link to the Tweebaa website and registers for membership…as long as their Tweebaa visit originated with your Share & Earn link you will be automatically credited with the referral.
